# lifeframework
go from large tasks to a step-by-step framework for success!
## how 2 use
put in all your major tasks or things you want to achieve on the left.

you can click the button where you'll get a textbox to put in any additional notes and stuff.

then, click the split tasks button, and AI will take your big tasks and split them into smaller, easy-to-follow chunks.
